Function Documentation¶

hal_status_t hal_init(void)

Initializes HAL services.

Performs lightweight HAL startup and logs the initialization banner.

Postconditions:

  • HAL services are available to callers.

Side effects:

  • Writes a log message.

Returns:

HAL_OK on success.
